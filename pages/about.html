Our Story

Maseno Every Finder was born out of a need to simplify the process of buying, selling, and finding hostels in the Maseno community. We noticed that students and residents were struggling with finding suitable accommodation and safe marketplaces.

  • Finding suitable hostels easily
  • Buying and selling items safely
  • Making secure payments via M-Pesa

Our platform addresses these challenges by providing a user-friendly, secure, and efficient solution for the Maseno community.

Our Mission

To create a seamless platform that connects the Maseno community, making it easier to buy, sell, and find accommodation while ensuring secure transactions and excellent user experience.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for community commerce and accommodation in Maseno, setting new standards for user experience and security in the region.
