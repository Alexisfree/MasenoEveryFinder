How It Works

Simple steps to get started with Maseno Every Finder. Choose your role and follow the guide.

Good news: The app is now available on the Play Store!

How to Buy Items

Simple steps to find what you need

1

Download & Register

Download the app and create your account using your student email. Complete the verification process.

2

Browse Items

Use the search and filter options to find items you're interested in. Save favorites for later.

3

Place Order

Submit your order through the app. Our admin team will review it and connect you with the seller.

4

Meet the Seller

Once connected, arrange a meeting with the seller at a safe location to inspect the item.

5

Payment & Delivery

After inspecting the item, make the payment directly to the seller upon delivery.

How to Sell Items

Turn your items into cash

1

Create Account

Sign up and verify your account to start selling items.

2

List Your Item

Take clear photos, write a detailed description, and set a fair price for your item.

3

Respond to Inquiries

Answer buyer questions promptly and negotiate prices if needed.

4

Complete Sale

Receive payment securely and arrange item pickup or delivery.

List Your Hostel

Reach more students easily

1

Prepare Info

Gather all necessary details about your hostel including photos, amenities, pricing, and room availability.

2

Contact Admin

Send your hostel details to our admin team for review and listing on the platform.

3

Verification

Our team will verify your information and prepare your hostel listing.

4

Go Live

Once approved, your hostel will be listed on the platform for students to discover.

Pro Tips

Stay Safe

Always meet in public places and verify the identity of the person you're dealing with.

Quality Photos

Take clear, well-lit photos of items from multiple angles to attract buyers.

Communication

Be prompt and professional in your communications to build trust.
