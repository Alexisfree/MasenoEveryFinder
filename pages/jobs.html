How it works

Bites connects campus vendors with students. As a delivery agent you accept or are assigned a delivery, pick up the order from the vendor, and deliver it to the student's hostel or room. We pay per completed delivery and record each payout in your rider dashboard.

Step-by-step

  1. Sign up: fill the short application (name, phone, ID, bike/foot).
  2. Onboard: quick verification (Student ID No) and short briefing.
  3. Receive jobs: delivery requests appear in your Rider dashboard.
  4. Accept & deliver: pick up from vendor, deliver to student, mark delivered.
  5. Get paid: your earnings update in realtime and are paid out weekly (or manually earlier).
